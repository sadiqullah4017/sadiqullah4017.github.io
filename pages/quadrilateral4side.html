  4. $$ \text{Area}(\triangle) = \frac{1}{2} \cdot a \cdot b \cdot \sin(C) $$ where: $$ \begin{align*} \text{Area}(\triangle) & : \text{Area of the triangle} \\ a & : \text{Length of side } a \\ b & : \text{Length of side } b \\ C & : \text{Angle opposite side } c \end{align*} $$
  5. \[ \text{Area}(\triangle) = \sqrt{s \cdot (s - a) \cdot (s - b) \cdot (s - c)} \] where \(s\) is the semi-perimeter, calculated as: \[ s = \frac{a + b + c}{2} \]
